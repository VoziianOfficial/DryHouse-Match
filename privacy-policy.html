Privacy Policy for DryHouse Match

This Privacy Policy describes how DryHouse Match collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit this website, submit a water-damage provider-matching request, contact us, or interact with the site.

DryHouse Match is an independent provider-matching platform. It is not a contractor, restoration company, repair company, inspection company, plumbing company, insurer, medical provider, or direct service provider.

Information We May Collect

When you submit a request form, we may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, selected service category, message details, source page, and consent confirmation.

Your message may include details about a water-damage situation, such as the affected room, visible water, possible source, water spread, moisture concerns, storm-related concerns, or other information you choose to provide.

We may also collect basic technical information through normal website operation, such as browser type, device type, pages viewed, approximate interaction time, and whether you accepted or declined the cookie notice.

How Information May Be Used

We may use submitted request information to understand your inquiry, organize your water-damage category, respond to your message, and help connect you with participating local providers where available.

Participating providers may use your submitted information to contact you about the request, discuss availability, ask follow-up questions, provide their own pricing information, explain scheduling options, or describe their service terms.

DryHouse Match does not set final pricing, scheduling, warranties, provider qualifications, licensing, insurance, work scope, or service terms. Those details are provided by participating providers.

How Information May Be Shared

We may share request information with participating providers for the purpose of helping you compare available local provider options. Sharing information does not guarantee provider availability, quote availability, response time, pricing, service terms, or project acceptance.

We may also share information when required by law, to protect website security, to prevent misuse, to respond to lawful requests, or to maintain website operations.

Cookies and LocalStorage

This site may use cookies or localStorage for essential preferences, including storing whether you accepted or declined the cookie notice. These tools help the site remember your preference so the notice does not repeatedly appear after you make a choice.

If analytics or similar tools are added in the future, they may use cookies or comparable browser storage to understand site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

For more detail, review the Cookie Policy.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a request form. You may also contact us to ask questions about your submitted information or request that we review, update, or delete information where applicable and legally permitted.

You can decline non-essential cookies through the cookie banner where available, and you can manage cookies or localStorage through your browser settings.

Security and Retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, transmission method, email system, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed completely secure.

We may retain request information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to requests, support provider-matching activity, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and operate the website.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking homeowner provider-matching information. It is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with revised content. Continued use of the website after updates means you acknowledge the updated policy.
