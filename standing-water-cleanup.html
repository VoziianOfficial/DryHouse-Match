Provider comparison factors

What to compare for standing water requests.

Before continuing with a provider, homeowners may want to ask about service area, timing, documentation, water source discussion, pricing approach, licensing, insurance, warranties, and what is included before any work begins.

Request details

Explain where water is visible.

Include the room, approximate affected area, possible source, whether water is spreading, and any flooring, walls, cabinets, or belongings involved.
