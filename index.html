Homeowner comparison clarity

Compare without confusing the platform with the provider.

DryHouse Match helps organize your request and may connect you with participating local providers. The platform does not perform the work, verify every provider claim, guarantee availability, or set final service terms.

  • Review provider availability for your area.
  • Ask providers about pricing, licensing, insurance, warranties, and timing.
  • Choose whether to continue after speaking with a provider.
  • Submitting a request does not create a service agreement.
