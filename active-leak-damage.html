Category fit guide

Is active leak the right request path?

Use this path when water appears to be entering, dripping, spreading, or showing up near ceilings, walls, fixtures, pipes, appliances, or roof-adjacent areas.

Compare all categories
Use this path when
  • 01

    Water is currently dripping, entering, or spreading inside the home.

  • 02

    Ceiling, wall, flooring, or surface marks appear to be growing.

  • 03

    Water appears near a pipe, fixture, appliance, or roof-adjacent area.

Choose another path when
  • 01

    Water is already standing across the floor — review Standing Water Cleanup.

  • 02

    The concern is mainly basement seepage — review Flooded Basement.

  • 03

    The issue is only odor, stains, or humidity — review Moisture & Mold Concerns.

Provider comparison factors

What to compare for active leak requests.

When reviewing provider options, homeowners may want to ask about service area, timing, inspection process, pricing approach, licensing, insurance, warranties, and what happens before any work begins.

Before choosing

Clarify the visible source area.

Describe where water appears to be entering, how quickly it is spreading, and which rooms or surfaces are affected before comparing provider options.

After reviewing options

Ask direct questions before continuing.

Discuss availability, pricing, site visit expectations, documentation, warranties, and service terms directly with any provider you consider.
