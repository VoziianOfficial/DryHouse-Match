Cookie Policy for DryHouse Match

This Cookie Policy explains how DryHouse Match may use cookies, localStorage, and similar browser technologies when you visit this website, submit a request, or interact with provider-matching pages.

DryHouse Match is an independent provider-matching platform. It is not a contractor, restoration company, inspection company, plumbing company, emergency service provider, or direct service provider.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. Similar technologies, such as localStorage, may store small pieces of information in your browser to remember preferences or improve website functionality.

Cookies and localStorage do not perform water-damage services, create a provider agreement, or guarantee provider availability. They are only browser technologies used by websites.

Types of Technologies We May Use

Essential storage

Used to support basic website functions, remember your cookie banner choice, and keep the site working as expected.

Preference storage

May remember whether you accepted or declined the cookie notice so the banner does not repeatedly appear after you make a choice.

Analytics storage

If analytics tools are added, they may help understand general website usage, visited pages, device type, and performance patterns.

Security and misuse prevention

Some technologies may help identify automated submissions, improve form reliability, reduce spam, and protect the website from misuse.

Cookie Banner Preference

This website may display a cookie notice. When you accept or decline the notice, your choice may be stored in localStorage so the website can remember your decision during later visits from the same browser.

Clearing browser storage, using a different browser, using private browsing mode, or using a different device may reset this preference and cause the notice to appear again.

Request Forms and Browser Storage

The provider-matching request form may use limited browser-side functionality to improve validation, reduce spam, and help confirm that required fields are completed before submission.

Form submission details are handled according to the Privacy Policy. Submitting a request does not create a service agreement with DryHouse Match or any participating provider.

Your Cookie Choices

You can accept or decline the cookie notice where the banner is available. You can also manage, block, or delete cookies and localStorage through your browser settings.

Blocking some technologies may affect certain website features, including preference storage, form validation behavior, banner display, or other basic functions.

Browser settings vary by browser and device. Review your browser’s help section for instructions on clearing cookies, blocking cookies, or managing localStorage.

Third-Party Tools

This website may use third-party libraries or services for fonts, icons, animation, or similar website functionality. These third parties may process technical information according to their own policies.

If analytics, advertising, call tracking, or additional third-party tools are added in the future, those tools may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own terms and privacy policies.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with revised content. Continued use of the website after updates means you acknowledge the updated Cookie Policy.
