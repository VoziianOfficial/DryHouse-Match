Before you compare

Prepare the details providers usually ask about your request.

A clear request helps you compare provider responses with less confusion. These simple details can make the next conversation easier before you decide whether to continue.

01

Take clear photos

Capture visible water, stains, ceiling marks, wall moisture, flooring, appliance areas, or basement water from more than one angle.

02

Note the exact location

Write down the room, surface, floor level, wall, ceiling, basement area, utility space, or nearby fixture connected to the concern.

03

Describe timing and status

Mention when you noticed the issue, whether water is still active, already stopped, spreading, drying, or only leaving visible marks.

04

Prepare provider questions

Ask about availability, pricing approach, visit fees, licensing, insurance, service terms, warranties, and who performs the work.
